## 6. Brand Visual Identity

### Logo / Icon
- **Concept:** A **clenched fist / power fist** graphic with lightning bolt elements — very bold and masculine
- **Color Palette:**
  - **Primary:** Deep **red/crimson** (#8B0000 range) — used for the company name "SUPERPOWER"
  - **Secondary:** **Black** — used for heavy outlines and the fist graphic
  - **Background:** **White/off-white** — used in the shirt/business card background
  - **Accent:** Red, white, and blue (American flag motif visible in cover art)
- **Typography on logo:** Bold, heavy, condensed sans-serif typeface — very impactful and industrial. The lightning bolt "S" or "⚡" is used as a stylized separator (e.g., "SUPER⚡POWER")
- **Overall brand feel:** Patriotic, strong, bold, blue-collar, no-nonsense

### Profile Photo (Facebook)
- A **white work van** with the phone number (609) 280-2284 on the door — vehicle branding shot
- Very practical, establishes trust and professionalism
